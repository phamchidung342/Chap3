Scrambles Eggs

Eggs are one my favorite foods. Here is a recipe deliciously rich scramble eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan oer a medium heat
  2. Genly mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs of the pan to the center every 20 saconds (as if are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook the plate until served)
